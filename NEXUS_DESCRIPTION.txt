[size=4][b]What does this mod do?[/b][/size]

When you're captured and waiting on the campaign map, press [b]F7[/b] to step into the scene — a live 3D mission populated with your captors, their soldiers, army lords, and fellow prisoners. Instead of staring at a menu, you're standing inside the castle dungeon or out in the field camp surrounded by the people who hold you.

The main purpose is immersive RP and [b]ChatAI group conversations[/b]: once inside you can open ChatAI's group menu (Alt+H / G) and have the captor lord, guards, companions, and other prisoners all talk to each other at once.

[size=4][b]Press F7 on the campaign map while captured[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][b]Held in a castle or town[/b] → Dungeon scene opens. You spawn inside a cell. Captor lords and visiting nobles walk the hallway outside, reachable through the bars.
[*][b]Held by a mobile party on the map[/b] → Outdoor camp scene opens. Your captors, their army companions, up to 6 troop guards, and fellow prisoners all appear in battle gear around you.
[/list]

[size=4][b]Who shows up in your camp scene[/b][/size]

[list]
[*]All heroes in the captor's direct party
[*]Lords from any army the captor belongs to
[*]Heroes and lord parties tracked in [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/mountandblade2bannerlord]FollowAll[/url] (soft dependency — works fine without it)
[*]Up to 6 troop soldiers as camp guards
[*]Other imprisoned heroes
[/list]

[size=4][b]Ransom suppression[/b][/size]

Vanilla's automatic ransom system gets in the way of long-form RP. This mod disables it entirely for the player:

[list]
[*]The AI will never queue a ransom decision for you
[*]The ransom offer panel never appears
[*]No code path can actually execute a ransom release on the player
[*]The "Propose ransom" option in the standard captivity menu is disabled with a tooltip
[/list]

NPC lords ransoming [i]each other[/i] is completely unaffected. Only your character is protected.

[size=4][b]Requirements[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][url=https://www.nexusmods.com/mountandblade2bannerlord/mods/2006]Bannerlord.Harmony[/url] — must be enabled and loaded above this mod
[/list]

[size=4][b]Optional (but recommended)[/b][/size]

[list]
[*][b]ChatAI[/b] — the main intended use case; group AI conversations in the spawned scene
[*][b]FollowAll[/b] — any heroes you're tracking will automatically appear in the camp scene
[/list]

[size=4][b]Installation[/b][/size]

[list=1]
[*]Extract so that [font=Courier New]PrisonerMenu/[/font] sits inside your [font=Courier New]Modules/[/font] folder
[*]Enable [b]Bannerlord.Harmony[/b] in the launcher (load it above Prisoner Menu)
[*]Enable [b]Prisoner Menu[/b] in the launcher
[*]Load your save, get captured, open the campaign map, press [b]F7[/b]
[/list]

Safe to add or remove mid-campaign — no save data is written.

[size=4][b]Compatibility[/b][/size]

[list]
[*]No XML edits — no load-order conflicts with other mods
[*]Does not touch combat, economy, diplomacy, or any gameplay system
[*]Ransom patches are narrowly scoped to [font=Courier New]Hero.MainHero[/font] only
[*]Works alongside AI Influence, AliceMM, ChatAI, FollowAll, and Player Switcher
[/list]
